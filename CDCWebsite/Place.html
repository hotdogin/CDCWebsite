These are my favorite places: the beach, Tagaytay, and the Enchanted Kingdom. These places are the places to go whenever you feel down, want to relax, or just want to have fun. The beach is one of my favorite places. I adore the sand and the water. The sun appears to be beaming constantly. The sky looks so pretty over the ocean, especially during sunrise and sunset. I also love Tagaytay. Aside from the cooler weather, this place is renowned for its abundance of spectacular natural views and diverse selection of business facilities. Last but not least, is the Enchanted Kingdom. I love this place because I can enjoy the rides and have fun with family and friends in a stress-free environment. It also offers a lot of exciting places because it not only offers a ride; Enchanted Kingdom offers performances like the musical. My favorite place is a place where I can feel joy, excitement, calmness, and relaxation because I really love a place where I have a peace of mind.
